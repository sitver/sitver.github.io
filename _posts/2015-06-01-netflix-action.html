We’ve seen shower-head speakers. We’ve seen creepy, autonomous speakers that look like trash cans. But we’ve never seen anything quite like this latest speaker, the sound torch.
 
This speaker is hot…literally. The Sound Torch is a portable bluetooth speaker with built-in pyrotechnics. Just hook up your music via bluetooth, and the sound torch automatically syncs to it, creating fiery patterns to match your fiery tunes.
 
The flames will jump as the base drops, sway to the chorus, or shoot and up and down to the beat, based on the settings you choose in the accompanying mobile app. You can create your own patterns, or use the pre-made patterns they provide.
 
The sound torch’s flames are powered by lighter fluid, and its tunes are powered by a 12-hour battery. It’s all being produced and sold by a dutch startup of the same name. 
 
The Sound Torch isn’t yet available to the average joe. It’s currently seeking funding on Kickstarter, to help start production. A previous attempt at crowdfunding failed earlier this year, but it looks like the sound torch may become a reality. 
 
So far they’ve raised 73% of their $30,000 goal, but according to Kickstarter-prediction site Kicktraq, they’re on pace to raise about 150% of their goal by the time their campaign closes in 16 days.
 
If you’d like your own Sound Torch, you can currently pre-order one for around $160 on Kickstarter today. Assuming that the company can truly produce a safe, functional product, they expect to ship Kickstarter orders by December of next year.
